# Amazon Interview Style Notes

## Coding
- Amazon uses Leadership Principles throughout technical rounds
- Expect problems focused on scalability and distributed systems
- Code quality and test coverage matter more than optimal Big O
- Often ask about trade-offs between different approaches

## Behavioral
- Amazon is heavily focused on Leadership Principles (14 LPs)
- Questions are almost always phrased around specific LPs
- "Tell me about a time when you disagreed with your manager"
- "Tell me about a time you took a calculated risk"
- STAR format is mandatory — interviewers will stop you and ask you to rephrase

## Difficulty Calibration
- Junior: LeetCode Easy-Medium
- Mid/Senior: LeetCode Medium
- Staff: LeetCode Medium-Hard + system design
